Mercedes-Benz G-Class Overview

The Mercedes-Benz G-Class, also known as the G-Wagon, is an iconic SUV that blends luxury, performance, and off-road capability. With a legacy spanning over four decades, the G-Class continues to be a symbol of status and power, appealing to adventure enthusiasts and elite buyers alike. Let's dive into a detailed review of its design, performance, features, and pricing.

The G-Class stays true to its boxy, rugged look while incorporating modern touches.

  • Bold front grille with Mercedes-Benz logo

  • Circular LED headlamps with DRLs

  • Massive 20-22 inch alloy wheels

  • Flared wheel arches for a muscular stance

  • Side-hinged rear door with a mounted spare wheel

  • Chrome and AMG styling options

The tough yet sophisticated exterior makes the G-Class instantly recognizable on and off the road.

The G-Class is available in multiple powertrains, offering both petrol and AMG performance variants.

Title Petrol Diesel
Model: Mercedes-AMG G 63 Mercedes-Benz G 400d
Engine: 4.0L V8 Bi-Turbo 3.0L, 6-cylinder turbo diesel
Power: 577 BHP 326 BHP
Torque: 850 Nm 700 Nm
Transmission: 9-speed AMG Speedshift 9-speed automatic
0-100 km/h: ~4.5 seconds ~6.4 seconds

With its 4MATIC all-wheel drive, three locking differentials, and high ground clearance, the G-Class dominates rough terrains with ease.

Despite its rugged build, the G-Class offers a premium and high-tech cabin.

  • Dual 12.3-inch widescreen displays S(Infotainment + Digital Instrument Cluster)

  • Burmester Surround Sound System

  • Nappa leather upholstery with wood & carbon fiber trims

  • Heated, ventilated, and massaging seats

  • Ambient lighting with 64 colour options

  • Wireless charging & smartphone connectivity

  • Multi-zone climate control

The spacious and plush interiors provide an ultra-luxurious driving experience.

Mercedes-Benz has equipped the G-Class with advanced safety and driver-assistance features.

  • 9 Airbags & Electronic Stability Control

  • Active Brake Assist & Blind Spot Assist

  • Lane-Keeping Assist & Adaptive Cruise Control

  • Off-Road ABS & Hill Descent Control

  • 360-degree Camera with Terrain View

Its advanced off-road capabilities, including low-range gearing and water-wading depth of 700mm, make the G-Class one of the best in its segment.

Is the G-Class Worth It?

  • ✅ Buy it if you want: Luxury, off-road dominance, and AMG performance

  • ❌ Skip it if you need: A budget-friendly or fuel-efficient SUV.

Final Verdict

The Mercedes-Benz G-Class is a dream SUV for those who want a powerful, luxurious, and iconic vehicle. While its price is premium, the status, performance, and off-road ability make it a top-tier choice.
