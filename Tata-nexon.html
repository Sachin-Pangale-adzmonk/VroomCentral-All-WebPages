Tata Nexon Overview

The Tata Nexon is one of India's most popular compact SUVs, known for its bold design, advanced safety features, and powerful engine options. Tata Motors has consistently improved the Nexon, making it a strong competitor in the sub-4-meter SUV segment. In this detailed blog, we’ll explore its specifications, features, mileage, price, and how it compares to its rivals.

The Nexon features a modern, futuristic design with its signature Humanity Line grille, LED DRLs, and coupe-like roofline. The 2023 facelift brings sharper headlights, a redesigned front bumper, and connected LED taillights, giving it an aggressive stance on the road.

  • LED Projector Headlamps with DRLs

  • Connected LED Tail Lamps with welcome animation

  • Dual-tone alloy wheels (16-inch)

  • Sporty Roof Rails

  • Bold & aerodynamic shape

The Tata Nexon offers both petrol and diesel engine options, catering to different driving needs.

Title Petrol Diesel
Engine: 1.2L Turbocharged Revotron Petrol 1.5L Turbocharged Revotorq Diesel
Power: 120 PS @ 5500 rpm 115 PS @ 3750 rpm
Torque: 170 Nm @ 1750-4000 rpm 260 Nm @ 1500-2750 rpm
Transmission: 6-speed Manual / AMT / 7-speed DCT 6-speed Manual / AMT
Mileage: 17-20 km/l 21-24 km/l

The multi-drive modes (Eco, City, and Sport) allow drivers to customize their driving experience based on road conditions.

The Nexon’s cabin is premium and feature-rich, offering a blend of comfort and technology.

  • 10.25-inch Touchscreen Infotainment System with Wireless Android Auto & Apple CarPlay

  • Fully Digital Instrument Cluster

  • Ventilated Front Seats (Top Variants)

  • Wireless Charging Pad

  • Premium Leatherette Upholstery

  • Electric Sunroof

  • Rear AC Vents

  • JBL Sound System (Top Variants)

The cabin space is generous, ensuring ample headroom and legroom, making it comfortable for long journeys.

Tata Nexon was the first Indian car to receive a 5-star safety rating from Global NCAP. The 2023 model continues to maintain high safety standards.

  • 6 Airbags (Top Variant)

  • Electronic Stability Control (ESC)

  • ABS with EBD

  • ISO-FIX Child Seat Mounts

  • 360-degree Camera

  • Front Parking Sensors

  • Hill Hold Assist & Hill Descent Control

Should You Buy the Tata Nexon?

The Tata Nexon is an excellent option if you are looking for:

  • A safe and well-built SUV with a 5-star rating

  • A feature-rich car with modern tech like a large touchscreen, digital cluster, and ventilated seats.

  • A car with both petrol and diesel options to suit your driving needs.

  • A stylish SUV with a bold and sporty design.

However, if you prefer better fuel efficiency, the Maruti Brezza might be a good alternative. If you want premium interiors, the Kia Sonet is worth considering.

Final Verdict

The Tata Nexon is an all-rounder that excels in safety, design, performance, and technology. With competitive pricing and top-tier features, it remains one of the best compact SUVs in India.
